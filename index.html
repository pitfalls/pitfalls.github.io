About

Background

I am a Senior Java Backend Developer and Team Lead with over seven years of professional experience, four of them in senior and lead positions. My career began with cryptographic, hardware-near programming at NXP Semiconductors R&D, where I worked within Hardware Security Module environments at chip level — from low-level C/C++ through Java-side integration.

Since 2022 I have been at XiTrust Secure Technologies, building and owning backend systems for MOXIS — an eIDAS/ETSI-compliant electronic signature platform processing several thousand signatures per month. I implemented signature handlers for qualified and simple electronic signatures, including integrations with two trust centres (Swisscom and A-Trust).

Beyond the core backend work, I have developed a genuine interest in applied AI integration: I designed and built an in-house AI agent system on Anthropic MCP that the engineering team uses daily, cutting structured ticket creation time by roughly 60%.

I am looking for the right opportunity where deep backend expertise and applied AI can make a real difference.
